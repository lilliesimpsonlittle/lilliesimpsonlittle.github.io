Surround Sound for VR Gaming

Here we have my new project, currently not finished however it is going to be a good one.

It is some wearable head gear that measures distance and is worn when in a room with surround sound (currently only working in 5.1). The idea is that the audio moves with you to provide a more immersive gaming experience in VR (and no headphones have to be worn). The whole thing (including the audio) is made using pure data, an arduino and a couple of unltrasonic sensors.
